Flight Data Management

I've developed a Flight Data Management System in C# for streamlined flight information management. It includes a secure login and registration system for user data privacy and access control. Once logged in, users can efficiently search for flights based on various criteria. They can also add new flights to the database with details like flight number, departure/arrival airports, times, and airline information. Notably, the system allows filtering flights by departure or arrival airports, simplifying tracking specific locations. Users can also remove irrelevant flights from the database. This system enhances efficiency and accuracy in flight information management, serving as a valuable tool for aviation professionals and enthusiasts.
